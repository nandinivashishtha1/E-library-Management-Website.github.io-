About Us

E Library Management System is a system which maintains the information about the books present in the library, their authors, the members of library to whom books are issued and all. This is very difficult to organize manually. Maintenance of all this information manually is a very complex task. Owing to the advancement of technology, organization of an E Library becomes much simple. The E Library Management has been designed to computerize and automate the operations performed over the information about the members, book issues and returns and all other operations. This computerization of library helps in many instances of its maintenances.
